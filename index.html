OhTae Jang

I am a Ph.D student at the POSTECH Intelligent Radar Signal Processing (IRAS) Laboratory, under the guidance of Prof. Kyungtae Kim, within the Department of Electrical Engineering at POSTECH.

My research interests lie in radar signal processing and SAR image analysis, with a particular focus on detection and recognition, clutter reduction, and domain generalization. I am especially interested in developing learning-based frameworks that bridge physical radar characteristics with modern machine learning techniques, including multimodal fusion and robust representation learning under extended operating conditions.

I am actively seeking opportunities for research collaborations and internships related to radar, remote sensing, and machine learning. Please feel free to reach out if you are interested in my work or would like to discuss potential collaborations.
