Profile

Oh-Tae Jang received the B.S. degree in electronic engineering from Yeungnam University, Gyeongsan, South Korea, in 2024. He is currently pursuing the M.S. degree in electrical engineering at the Intelligent Radar Signal Processing (IRAS) Laboratory, POSTECH, Pohang, South Korea. His research interests include radar signal processing, detection and recognition for SAR imagery, multimodal fusion, radar-based indoor monitoring, and domain generalization.
